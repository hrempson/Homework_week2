About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Heather Empson, born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. I graduated with a bachelors degree in Psychology from Arizona State University in 2012 (Go Sun Dev's!!). Right out of college, I began my career in technical recruiting at a small firm in Scottsdale, Arizona. After a couple of years in the industry, I continued my career as an in-house corporate recruiter at Intel, which later lead to a role at Microsoft, which lead to my current role as a technical recruiter at Google.

After spending 8+ years of experience working with many software and hardware development teams and helping them hire talent, I found myself curious to learn more. I wanted to know how I could better understand my product groups and the technical jargan that fills resumes and job descriptions I see on a daily basis. Ultimately, gaining this knowledge could help me better match candidates to the the right roles and properly prepare them for technical interviews. That curiousity led me to enroll in a coding bootcamp at the University of Washington and here I am! This site has some links to projects that I've been working on over my 6 month bootcamp journey. Feel free to browse over to my Portfolio page to have a look!
